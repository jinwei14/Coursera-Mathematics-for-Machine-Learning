# Inner Product: Length

$$||X|| = \sqrt{<x,x>}$$

- The length of $||X||$ is also called the norm of $X$
- If we stretch a vector $X$ with a constant $\lambda$ then

$$||\lambda X||=|\lambda| ||X||$$

## Triangle inequality

$$||x+y|| \leq ||x|| + ||y||$$

## Cauchy Schwarz Inequality

$$||<x,y>|| \leq ||x||\text{ }||y||$$
